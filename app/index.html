About Me

I am a Unity Developer specialized in immersive technologies, with over 3 years of experience developing interactive applications, VR training simulators, and augmented reality experiences.

My work focuses on building real-time 3D solutions that help companies communicate ideas, train teams, and showcase products through engaging and interactive environments. I have participated in the development of enterprise VR simulators, web-based AR experiences, and virtual worlds with real-time networking.

I enjoy solving technical challenges, optimizing performance for immersive devices, and creating intuitive interaction systems that enhance user experience. I am always interested in exploring new technologies in XR, interactive storytelling, and real-time 3D development.

Based in Bogota, Colombia — available for remote work and freelance projects.
